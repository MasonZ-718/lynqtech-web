ReAnchor

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

Short version: Everything you enter stays on your device. ReAnchor does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

What we collect

ReAnchor does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, email address, or any identifying information to function.

Where your data lives

All decisions, notes, and settings you create are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and iOS Keychain (for premium status). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Third-party services

ReAnchor uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — processes in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect transaction metadata (purchase date, product ID) as required to complete the transaction. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry — receives anonymous crash reports if the app encounters an unhandled error. Crash reports may include device type, OS version, and app version. No personal data or decision content is ever included. See Sentry's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — your App Store purchase history and device identifiers are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, ReAnchor schedules local reminders on your device. These notifications are processed entirely on-device and are not sent to any server.

Children's privacy

ReAnchor is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
